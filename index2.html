Lozova

The city where you were born and raised has its own special atmosphere. Lozova is a city of open and sincere people, a city of beautiful streets and cozy parks, modern sports grounds, and comfortable parks and squares. Lozova is one of the main industrial centers and railway junctions of the Sloboda Ukraine region, a green, flowering, native city. It has well-developed machine-building, light, and food industries. Lozova is a sports city with the largest skate park in the Kharkiv region (excluding the city of Kharkiv), very good sports grounds for football and volleyball with artificial and grass coverings, as well as a rope park.

DYUSH "Yunost" is a sports organization that prepares children and youth for participation in sports competitions and contributes to their development in this direction. It is here that children and adolescents have access to classes in various sports, including football, volleyball, basketball, athletics, and others. All coaches who work in the organization are qualified specialists who help students achieve the best results in sports competitions. In addition, DYUSH "Yunost" regularly holds sports competitions and tournaments with other sports clubs, which helps to develop sports culture and a healthy lifestyle among young people in Lozova.

Lozova is a city of science and education. 25 educational institutions operate in one of the largest territorial communities in Ukraine. However, not all survived, on September 16, 2022, a missile strike was carried out on the state institution "Lozivsky Lyceum No. 8", as a result of which it was completely destroyed.

Lozova is a city of robotics. The "Laboratory of Interesting Ideas" robotics club operates in the city, where everyone from 6 to 16 years old can master the basics of robotics, programming, and electronics.

Lozova is a city of culture and creativity. The pride of the city and a gathering place for creative people was the Palace of Culture, which was put into operation in 1977. From 2019 to 2021, the building underwent a major renovation. On May 20, 2022, in broad daylight, the establishment was destroyed by an enemy missile.

Lozova is a city of remarkable people. Lozova is famous for its citizens who glorify our city and our country to the whole world. These include Mykola Kucherenko (one of the designers of the T-34 tank), Oleksandr Hladkyi (footballer), Leonid Sirko (singer), and many other equally well-known figures. Of course, Lozova is proud of its military, who protect our peace and our lives around the clock.

Lozova is a city in eastern Ukraine that always welcomes both a casual tourist and desired guests. Come and you will definitely fall in love with our city, you won't even notice how a smile appears on your face and you will feel light in your soul, as if you have come home.
